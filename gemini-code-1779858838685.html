Optimizing Live Tool Holders for Swiss-Type Lathes: Vibration Control in High-Speed Machining

走心式車床高速加工下的「動力刀座」選型與震動抑制指南

When machining high-precision medical bone screws or micro electronic components, engineers operating TSUGAMI, STAR, or Citizen Swiss-type lathes often encounter a critical bottleneck: micro-vibration. When the spindle speed ramps up beyond 6,000 RPM for micro-drilling (<3mm), unexpected vibration severely degrades surface roughness (Ra) and dramatically shortens tool life.

在加工醫療骨釘或精密電子零件時，許多操作 TSUGAMI（津上）、STAR（星辰） 等走心式車床的工程師都會遇到一個棘手問題：當主軸轉速拉高到 6,000 RPM 以上進行微孔加工時，動力刀座出現的細微震動，常導致工件表面粗糙度（Ra）超標，甚至頻繁斷刀。

⚡ High-Speed Speeders: The Double-Edged Sword

As indexed in our technical library, gear-multiplier speeders boost turret drive RPM by 2x to 5x. However, many procurement teams overlook the impact of gear backlash. In a recent case with a South Korean automotive tier-1 supplier, we replaced standard off-the-shelf components with Z&Z STROTEC's custom live tools featuring ground gears and ceramic bearings, successfully reducing micro-vibration by 40%.

高轉速增速刀座的雙面刃： 增速刀座（Gear-multiplier）能將轉速提升 2x 至 5x，是微孔加工的利器。然而實務上許多採購忽略了齒輪背隙（Backlash）的影響。我們曾協助一家韓國汽車零件大廠，將原本市售劣質的齒輪結構，更換為採用萬洋客製化研磨齒輪與陶瓷軸承（Ceramic Bearings）的動力刀座，成功將微震動降低了 40%，刀具壽命直接延長 1.8 倍。

🔧 Machine Compatibility vs. Machining Stability

Ensuring a BMT or VDI interface matches your turret specification is only the first step. For high-precision operations, the precision of the drive coupling (polygon, square, or round) dictates the actual stability. To safeguard our European and Swiss clients' operations, every single live tool exported by Z&Z STROTEC undergoes rigorous dynamic balancing tests to guarantee zero eccentric runout at maximum RPM.

介面相容性不等於加工穩定性： 很多採購以為只要 BMT 或 VDI 介面尺寸對了就萬事大吉。但在精密加工中，驅動聯軸器（Drive coupling）的貼合度才是關鍵。萬洋國際在出貨給瑞士與歐洲高階客戶時，都會額外進行動平衡（Dynamic Balancing）測試，確保出廠的每一把刀座在極速運轉下依然穩定、絕無偏心跳動。
